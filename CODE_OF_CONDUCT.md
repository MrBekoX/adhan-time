# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in
the Adhan Time community a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity. We commit to acting in ways that foster
an open, friendly, and healthy community.

## Our standards

We expect everyone to be respectful and considerate, to welcome differing
viewpoints and constructive feedback, and to focus on what is best for the
community and the project. Disrespectful, demeaning, or unwelcome conduct
toward others is not acceptable.

## Adopted standard

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its full code
of conduct, including its detailed standards and enforcement guidelines. The
complete text is available at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and
may take appropriate, fair corrective action in response to behavior they deem
inappropriate.

## Reporting

To report a concern, contact the maintainer privately through GitHub
([@MrBekoX](https://github.com/MrBekoX)) — for example via the repository's
**Security → Report a vulnerability** form, which also routes private messages
to the maintainer. All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
